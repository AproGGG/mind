Mind of Science debates

Mind of science (MOS) debates is a scientific competition that aims to unite the Azerbaijani youth by the means of trustful debating, critical thinking, and exploring the lovely, progressing world of science. The event features a total of three committees with their own general agendas and topics, where the members of each committee, called ‘debaters’, are expected to bring out the most suitable and effective solution to the pre-given topic in teams while representing a specific organization. The further process will be evaluated by appointed committee judges...
